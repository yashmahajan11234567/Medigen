# MediGen - Page 5: Medical Records

## Vision
Medical Records is MediGen's healthcare document hub. It organizes treatment episodes into records (folders) while linking prescriptions, pharmacy bills, schedules, inventory items and generic medicine searches.

### Version 1
- Create/Rename/Delete Medical Records
- Upload Prescription
- Upload Pharmacy Bill
- Upload Other Documents
- Notes
- Grid/List view
- Search
- Links to Schedule, Inventory and Generic Searches

### Version 2
- OCR extraction
- Auto-fill prescription details
- Smart document classification
